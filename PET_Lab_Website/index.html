Pipeline

Five steps. ESM-2 language model. Gradient-boosted scoring.

Collect known proteins

Pull real structures from RCSB PDB and AlphaFold — 200,000+ structures worldwide.

Understand with ESM-2

Meta's 650M-parameter protein language model reads each sequence and maps residue contributions to function.

Score every mutation

6,000+ amino acid swaps scored per sequence for thermal stability, catalytic activity, and PET binding affinity.

Combine and rank

Top mutations combined into multi-mutation candidates, verified by a gradient boosting ensemble at ~80% cross-validated accuracy.

Output for the lab

Ranked candidates with full protein and DNA sequences — ready for gene synthesis and experimental validation.

Our Mission

PET Lab is an AI-powered enzyme engineering platform focused on the global plastic waste crisis. We design thermostable PETase variants for industrial-scale recycling — optimizing catalytic activity, thermal stability, and PET binding at the temperatures real recycling plants operate at. By fusing protein language models with gradient-boosted mutation scoring, we compress years of wet-lab iteration into seconds of compute.
